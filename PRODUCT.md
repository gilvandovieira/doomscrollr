# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Doomscrollr is for mobile users who create and share SFW meme-like posts with friends, primarily
through WhatsApp. They are often arriving from a shared link, reading quickly on a phone, and
deciding whether to react, comment, share, or make their own post.

## Product Purpose

Doomscrollr v1 validates one loop: create a post, share it to WhatsApp, have a friend open it, react
or comment, and bring the creator back. Success is measured by real post creation, shared opens,
reactions, comments, and repeat creation, not by broad social-platform scope.

## Brand Personality

Fast, rough-cut, and social. The product should feel meme-native and opinionated without making
common mobile actions feel strange, fragile, or over-designed.

## Anti-references

Avoid a full desktop brutalist poster system applied directly to mobile controls. Avoid generic
infinite-feed social clones, ad-heavy meme sites, crypto/terminal aesthetics, polished lifestyle
gradients, and any interface that makes WhatsApp sharing feel secondary.

## Design Principles

Put the share loop in thumb reach.

Keep the feed readable before it is expressive.

Preserve a rough, meme-board edge without making every control shout.

Treat post pages as the conversion moment: open, understand, react, comment, share.

Earn every feature with v1 validation behavior.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for core text and controls. Support keyboard focus, reduced motion, readable
mobile type, large touch targets, and clear signed-out states that preserve public read access.
